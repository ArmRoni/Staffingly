Q.Do you work with designers and brands?

Q.What is your production capacity?

A.Our factory has the capability to handle both small-scale and large-scale production orders, maintaining high standards of craftsmanship and quality control.

Q.What are your lead times for production?

A.Lead times can vary depending on the complexity and size of the order. Generally, we strive to maintain efficient production schedules and communicate timelines clearly with our clients.

A.Yes, we offer bespoke manufacturing services where clients can customize designs, materials, and finishes to meet their specific requirements.

Q.What materials do you use?

A.We utilize premium leathers primarily sourced from Bangladesh, with additional imports from Italy and Japan as per customer requirements. Our metal accessories are sourced from renowned suppliers in Japan and China.

Q.Where are your products manufactured?

A.Our products are proudly manufactured in Bangladesh, known for its tradition of artisanal leather craftsmanship.

Q.Do you work with sustainable practices?

Q.What quality control measures do you have in place?

A.We have rigorous quality control processes at every stage of production, ensuring that each piece meets our high standards before it leaves our factory.
