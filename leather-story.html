LEATHER STORY

Bangladesh’s Rich Livestock Tradition Fuels Leather Industry

Bangladesh's fertile alluvial soil produces exceptional animal hides known for their fine grain and smooth texture, ensuring a steady supply of top-quality full-grain leather.

The country's deep-rooted livestock farming tradition supports both household needs and commercial purposes, with beef, goat, lamb, and buffalo meat meeting domestic demand.

This vibrant sector provides a steady raw hide supply, with 70% collected during Eid-ul-Adha and the rest year-round

Despite 12%-18% loss due to poor collection methods, we aim to minimize waste by turning these hides into premium leather products through sustainable practices.

Cowhide makes up 70% of leather production, goat skin 20%, and buffalo and sheep hides the remainder.

ART OF LEATHER CRAFTING

Bangladesh’s Leather Sector: A Global Force

With a 3% contribution to the world’s leather industry, Bangladesh stands out for its exquisite fine-grain leather. Known globally for its consistent fiber structure, luxurious smoothness, and rich natural texture, it’s a mark of unparalleled quality

Bangladesh’s leather sector thrives after meeting local demands, with exports mainly reaching China, Hong Kong, and Italy. For leather footwear, the United States, Germany, and the Netherlands are key markets.

Environmentally Responsible Production

To ensure eco-friendly leather production, the Bangladesh government has established a dedicated zone in Savar with a state-of-the-art Central Effluent Treatment Plant (CETP).

This initiative relocates industries from Hazaribagh to Savar, prioritizing pollution control and improved working conditions.

Commitment to Quality & Sustainability

Our leather is sourced from certified tanneries that uphold the highest environmental and labor standards, ensuring worker safety, fair wages, and health benefits.

Each purchase supports sustainability, offering beautifully crafted leather goods that make a difference.

LEATHER TYPE

Crafting Excellence with Premium Leather

Milling  Leather

Experience unmatched elegance with our flawless leather perfect for handbags, wallets & card cases. Its smooth, bump-free surface and lightweight softness redefine luxury and versatility.

Embossed Leather

WCrafted for lasting durability, this leather boasts a finely treated surface that minimizes scratches and repels dirt. Ideal for wallets and bags, it ensures a flawless appearance over time.

Oiled Leather

Enhanced with 10-15% more oil than standard tanned leather, this material enhances durability, water resistance, and ages beautifully. Ideal for crafting wallets and bags.

Waterproof and water-repellent leather

Water-resistant leather is durable and naturally textured, ideal for backpacks, tote bags, wallets, and zip wallets. Choose waterproof leather for full protection or water-repellent leather for a sleek, moisture-resistant finish.

Exploring Global Leather: A World of Options

Discover Japanese Leather

WExperience the timeless beauty of Japanese leather, renowned for its rich texture and artisanal craftsmanship. Vegetable-tanned for lasting durability, this luxurious material develops a unique patina over time, making it the perfect choice for high-end wallets, belts, and bags.

Elevate Your Style with Italian Leather

Leather from Italy boasts exceptional quality and unique character. Made from full-grain leather and uniquely vegetal-tanned, it develops a rich patina over time. This premium material blends traditional craftsmanship with modern elegance, ideal for luxury leather goods.

ABOUT TANNING

Crafting Quality Leather

For centuries, leather has been valued for its durability, versatility, and timeless elegance. The art of tanning transforms raw hides into premium leather, preserving them and enhancing their unique qualities. At BLJ Bangladesh, we utilize expert tanning techniques to craft exceptional bags, wallets, shoes, and leather accessories, ensuring quality and longevity in every product.

Tanning

Vegetable tanning

Embrace sustainability with our eco-friendly leather crafted using natural vegetable tannins and free from harmful chemicals. Our commitment to green manufacturing ensures a product made entirely from natural materials.

Chrome tanning

We provide chrome-tanned leather: a supple, durable material that maintains its quality over time and requires minimal upkeep. Ideal for effortless elegance and resilience.

Chrome-free tanning

Chrome-free leather is an eco-friendly alternative to traditional tanning methods, offering both durability and a luxurious, soft texture. Its versatile nature makes it ideal for a variety of products, from accessories to bags.
