Manufacturing Services

Design Development & Evaluation:

We provide expert guidance in the design development and evaluation of leather goods, wallets, bags, hand bags & shoe’s ensuring your vision is realized.

Premium Leather Sourcing:

We source the highest quality leathers from Bangladesh, Japan, and Italy for our leather bags, backpacks, tote bags, clutch bags, zip wallets, sneakers.

Prototype Creation:

Our team crafts precise designs and prototypes for bucket bag, duffel bags, hobo, wallets, travel accessories, loafers & boots transforming your ideas into tangible products.

Artisanal Craftsmanship:

Each leather wallet, satchel bag, shoulder bag & sandals showcases the blend of traditional craftsmanship with handcrafted perfection and modern design.

Mastery in Leather Goods, Japanese Artistry in Every Touch

At our Bangladesh leather factory, artisans trained by Japanese leather masters craft exceptional leather goods with generations of expertise in leather goods manufacturing. From leather bags to wallets, every piece reflects timeless artistry and superior craftsmanship.
