Company Overview

BLJ Bangladesh Corporation Ltd. specializes in manufacturing high quality leather goods and exclusively exports its products globally. Since 2013, we have been a part of Borderless Japan Corporation and have operated in Bangladesh with the dual mission of producing premium leather goods and addressing pressing social issues.

We are dedicated to creating employment opportunities for vulnerable and marginalized groups, including the unemployed, physically challenged individuals, single mothers, third genders, orphans, and assault victims.

Over the past 11 years, we supply leather products to more than 90 buyers in Japan, Europe, and other countries, consistently meeting their expectations with our superior quality products.

Our Factory

At our factory, a dedicated team of 650 skilled professionals and operate with over 140 state of the art machines, including 76 sewing machines, 21 leather cutting machines, 28 leather skiving machines, 2 leather splitting machines, 4 logo embossing and stamping machines, and additional equipment like leather measurement and zipper cutting machines.

With these resources, we are equipped to produce a diverse range of leather products from bags, handbags, backpacks to tote bags, wallets, card holders, leather accessories, travel accessories, and footwear.

Whether your needs are for large scale production or bespoke, small scale orders, we deliver high-quality craftsmanship tailored to your specifications.
