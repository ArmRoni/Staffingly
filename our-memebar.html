Mohammed Kabir

Profile

Hi, my name is Mohammed Kabir, a business graduate from the University of Greenwich, London, UK. I had lived in London for five years, where I was a part of a colorful mix of cultures that enriched my communication skills. After my time in london, I joined BLJ Bangladesh Corporation Ltd in 2015. Since then I learned about the process of leather goods manufacturing, production management, QC control & export. Apart from that, I learned about sourcing & importing the best quality raw materials from abroad. All these experiences equipped me with the skills to be professional merchandiser to get in touch with buyers globally. Now I’m serving here as the Manager of sales & marketing.

Message

From the beginning, besides our bulk production; we’re working with our OEM & ODM buyers, whatever they ordered our minimum quantity. We strongly believe that we will become successful only when our buyers become successful. Therefore we are committed to ensuring 100% quality control, effective communication, on-time shipment, to our valuable buyers globally.
