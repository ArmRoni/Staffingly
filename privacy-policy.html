Privacy Policy

BLJ Bangladesh Corporation Limited (hereinafter referred to as “the Company”) defines its policies on handling personal information in the services provided on the website “BLJ LEATHER OEM” https://en.bljoem.com/ (hereinafter referred to as “this site”).

1.Handling of personal information

Our purpose of using personal information
In business activities related to BLJ Bangladesh (business name), the Company will acquire personal information in a fair and appropriate manner, and use it within the necessary scope of achieving the following purposes:

  • (1) To provide products and services handled by the Company
  • (2) To respond to customer inquiries
  • (3) To inform users about our services
  • (4) To deliver information, surveys, and other information related to our business
  • (5) To ask for opinions and feedback regarding our services and products
  • (6) Credit management, collection management, and claims management related to transactions with our customers

If we find it necessary to use personal information obtained from customers for purposes other than as listed above, we will do so after acquiring the consent of the individual. In addition, the Company will clearly designate its personal information administrator and take specific measures to prevent unintended use.

2.Provision of personal information from customers

Provision of personal information from customers (including written forms, entering on the web, and oral provision) is optional. However, if we cannot acquire necessary information from our customers, or if we are provided with false or incorrect information, we may not be able to correctly respond to their requests.

3.Provision of personal information to third parties

Personal information that we have acquired from customers will not be provided or disclosed to third parties except in the following cases.

  • (1) When prior consent is obtained from the customer
  • (2) When you must provide it based on laws and regulations
  • When personal information is provided following a business succession due to a merger or other reasons
  • (4) In cases where a national institution or local public entity or a person entrusted with it requires it to carry out the affairs stipulated by laws and regulations, and obtaining the consent of the person in question will hinder the execution of the affairs
  • (5) When there is an inquiry from a credit card company to investigate unauthorized card use

4.Consignment of personal information

We may outsource the handling of all or part of the personal information we have acquired from our customers to the extent necessary for our purpose. In such a case, we will form a contract that ensures the secure and confidential handling of personal information with the contractor, and will appropriately manage and oversee the contractor.

5.Notification of the purpose of use of personal information

If the customer requests a disclosure of the purpose of using their personal information or a disclosure of the contents of personal information based on the provisions of the Personal Information Protection Law, the Company shall confirm the requester’s identity and promptly provide help.
However, this does not apply if any of the following applies:

  • (1) If you cannot verify your identity
  • (2) When the personal information held by the Company could not be specified according to the details of the request
  • (3) When the Company is not obligated to disclose due to the Personal Information Protection Law or other laws
  • (4) When there is a risk of harming the life, body, property, or other rights and interests of the requestor or a third party
  • (5) When there is a risk of hindering the proper operation of our business
  • (6) When it violates the law

If we are unable to provide help, we will promptly notify the requester of the reasons. To request a notification of the purpose of use, please contact us through the following [Inquiry for personal information].

6.Disclosure, correction, addition and deletion of personal information

If you wish to disclose, correct, add, or delete your personal information (hereinafter referred to as “correct, etc.”), we will respond after confirming your identity. We will promptly provide help if we determine that it is necessary.
However, disclosure, correction, etc. will not be possible if any of the following applies.

  • (1) When there is a risk of harming the life, body, property, or other rights and interests of the requester or a third party
  • (2) When there is a risk of significant hindrance to the proper operation of our business
  • (3) When it violates the law
  • (4) In case the personal information entrusted to us by a third party did not include permission to disclose it
  • (5) When the Company is not obligated to disclose, correct, etc. due to the Personal Information Protection Law or other laws As a general rule, we do not accept requests from family members to disclose or correct a customer’s personal information. However, this does not apply if the Company deems necessary.

In responding to disclosing, correcting, etc. personal information, we may charge an extra fee. If we are unable to provide help, we will promptly notify you of the reason.

For requests for disclosure, correction, etc. regarding your personal information, please contact the Personal Information Inquiry Office below.

7.Right to refuse to provide or the use of personal information

If you refuse to provide or the use of your personal information and we find it necessary to respond, we will respond promptly after confirming your identity. However, we cannot respond to cases that fall under items (1) to (5) in “7. Disclosure, correction, addition and deletion of personal information”. We will notify you without delay if we stop providing or using your personal information. If we are unable to fulfill your request, we will promptly notify you of the reason.

Please contact us through the following [Inquiry for personal information] for requests for suspension of provision or use.

8.Appropriate management of personal information and risk management

The Company strictly manages a series of management processes from acquiring personal information to disposing of it, and takes appropriate preventive measures against risks such as leakage, loss, or damage of personal information (including unauthorized access, loss, destruction, and alteration). In the unlikely event that personal information is at risk, we will quickly grasp the situation and take corrective action. In addition, we will make all employees aware of the importance of protecting personal information and work to manage personal information appropriately.

9.Privacy policy change procedure

The Company shall review the operational status regarding the handling of personal information from time to time and strive for continuous improvement, and may change the personal information protection policy as necessary. However, if the customer’s consent is required by law, the Company will acquire the customer’s consent by the method outlined by the Company.

10.Compliance with laws, national guidelines and other standards

In handling personal information, we acquired, we will comply with laws and regulations, national guidelines, and other standards regarding personal information protection

◯ Cookies

Cookies are a mechanism that identifies the computer used by the customer and records the information selected by the customer on the hard drive of the computer, so that the user can use the website more conveniently on their next visit. However, cookies do not identify you personally. If you set to refuse cookies, you may not be able to fully utilize the website.

◯ Access log

When a customer accesses a website, their activities are recorded as an access log. This log does not contain personally identifiable information. We collect information to improve the convenience of the website, to investigate the cause of a problem, and to perform statistical analysis on usage. It is not used for any other purpose.

◯ Link

There may be links to third-party websites on this website, but please note that the handling of personal information on linked websites is outside of the scope of our responsibility. We encourage you to check the privacy policy provided by the linked website
