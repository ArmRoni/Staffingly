STEP 1

Understanding Your Vision

we transform your ideas into artful,handcrafted bags, wallets, and travel accessories, blending tradition with innovation. Every piece reflects your unique style and vision, crafted to perfection.

STEP 2

Request a Quote

Request a personalized quote based on our consultation customized to your specific needs. Let us create a custom solution that aligns perfectly with your vision and project requirements.

STEP 3

Sample Production (7 to 14 Days)

After our meeting, we will create a detailed sample to realize your vision. Together, we'll refine the specifications by assessing the sample's elements, ensuring alignment with your expectations and requirements for optimal results.

STEP 4

Production (30 days to 45 days)

Specializing in high-quality leather goods, we guarantee timely delivery without compromising craftsmanship. Our expert team and efficient processes ensure precision, consistency, and reliability in every order.

STEP 5

Quality Control Excellence for Leather Goods

Experience unparalleled quality through our rigorous inspection and quality control processes: leather marking before cutting, thorough checks before distribution, in-line QC before and after sewing, and a final inspection to ensure excellence in every piece.

STEP 6

Exquisite Packing for Exquisite Products

We believe that great leather deserves great packaging. Our thoughtful packing process guarantees that each piece of leather craftsmanship is delivered to you in impeccable condition, ready to impress.

STEP 7

Flexible Shipping Options to Suit Your Needs

Choose from our air or sea freight services to fit your timeline and budget. Air freight delivers in just one week, while sea freight offers a more economical option with a delivery time of up to two months. Select the best solution for your needs and delivery goals..

Sustainable Initiatives

ATTEMPT

POINT 01

①Empowering the Underserved

We transform lives by offering employment to those struggling to find work. By prioritizing opportunities for the unemployed, physically challenged, single mothers, third genders, orphans, and survivors of violence, we’ve grown from a modest start of two individuals to a thriving factory that now supports around 1,000 people.

POINT 02

②Committed to Sustainability

DIN EN ISO 14001:2015 – Environmental management systems certified, We primarily utilize eco-friendly vegetable-tanned leather, alongside chrome-tanned and chrome-free options, as well as recyclable and biodegradable materials, ensuring our processes protect the planet. 

POINT 03

③Upcycling Local Resources

During the annual Muslim festival "Eid," cows are offered in tradition, leaving behind valuable cowhide, goat hide after the feast. Instead of letting this resource go to waste, we upcycle the cowhide, transforming it into premium leather goods.
