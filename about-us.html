Mission & Vision

Redefining Excellence with Sustainable Innovation

Mission

At BLJ Bangladesh Corporation Ltd., our mission is to transform social challenges into opportunities for growth. We are dedicated to improving employment prospects for marginalized groups including the unemployed, physically challenged individuals, single mothers, third genders, orphans, deaf and survivors of assault. By championing entrepreneurial initiatives, we strive to create lasting, positive change and uplift those who need it most.

Vision

We aspire to set the global standard in manufacturing excellence. Our vision is to transform the factory environment into a thriving community, enriched with top-tier living conditions, educational opportunities, and childcare facilities. We are dedicated to not only creating exceptional job opportunities but also cultivating a distinguished work culture that exemplifies global standards of excellence and integrity.

Why We

Elevate Every Detail Expertly Crafted Leather Goods

At BLJ Bangladesh Corporation, we merge innovation with tradition to deliver unparalleled leather goods. Our expert team excels in design & evaluation, using premium quality leather materials to craft bespoke designs and prototypes. Certified to ISO 14001:2015 standards, we are committed to sustainable practices and environmental responsibility in all aspects of our operations.

ur artisanal manufacturing ensures meticulous attention to detail, allowing us to offer small quantity orders with precise quality control in production. Collaborating with Japanese expert artisans and LWG-certified suppliers, we guarantee the highest standards in craftsmanship and sustainability, using eco-friendly materials for a responsible luxury experience.

Fair Trade Commitment

Ethical Elegance Fair Trade Leather Craftsmanship in Every Stitch

At our core, we champion Fair Trade principles by ensuring fair wages and safe working conditions for all. Our dedication to transparency and accountability fosters trust, while our support for local artisans and underrepresented producers empowers communities.

We invest in capacity building and training, promotes diversity and inclusion, and gives back through meaningful community initiatives. Committed to sustainability and women’s empowerment, we strive to create lasting impact and opportunities for all.

Employee Benefits and Well being

Excellence in Leather Goods with Employee Centric Care

At BLJ Bangladesh Corporation Ltd., we prioritize our employees well-being with a comprehensive range of benefits. Female employees receive complimentary sanitary napkins. Every member has the option to retain up to 8 days of earned leave with monetary compensation. We reward punctuality with attendance bonuses and ensure timely salary disbursement within the first 8 business days of each month.

Our commitment to work-life balance includes two festival bonuses annually, maternity leave of four months at full salary and access to on-site medical services including weekly consultations with an MBBS doctors and 24/7 nursing care for all members. We support families with a monthly child allowance from 6 months of age 1 year and offer educational scholarships at various levels. Our on-site daycare provides a safe and nurturing environment, allowing working parent’s peace of mind.

Employees can benefit from interest-free loans from the welfare fund & up to 75% of their provident fund balance with an interest rate of 6% and receive medical insurance coverage of up to 30,000 BDT annually, with additional group insurance benefits and a death benefit of 500,000 BDT & Additionally, each employee benefits from comprehensive group insurance coverage of 100,000 BDT, offering valuable financial security and support. Our partnerships with leading hospitals like Labaid, Ibn Sina, and Razu General Hospital ensure exceptional medical care at minimal cost.

Compliance

Commitment to Compliance, Safety, and Equality

We are committed to upholding the highest ethical standards by strictly adhering to all relevant laws, consumer protection guidelines, and environmental policies. Our operations are designed to minimize environmental impact, with an Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) and Environmental Management Plan (EMP) in place. Our factory ensures a safe and comfortable environment with regulated noise levels, proper ventilation, and temperature control.

We provide clean drinking water, separate restrooms, and first aid facilities for all. We promote gender sensitivity, equity in promotions, and comprehensive counseling services for all employees. A health and safety committee is established to ensure continuous compliance with legal standards and to foster a fair, inclusive workplace.

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

Building a Better Future

At BLJ Bangladesh Corporation Ltd., our CSR efforts reflect our commitment to creating positive change. We drive poverty alleviation through impactful social enterprise initiatives and empower women by offering employment opportunities to widows, divorcees, elderly women, and single mothers.

Our dedication to environmental stewardship is evident in our tree planting programs, while our support for community engagement includes regular sports and cultural activities. We are committed to disaster relief across the nation and promote education through various initiatives. Additionally, our comprehensive healthcare programs include a free blood donation service for emergencies, and we nurture entrepreneurship by actively supporting budding entrepreneurs.
